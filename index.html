I am a PhD student at Université Gustave Eiffel , working under the supervision of Alfredo Hubard and Arnaud de Mesmay .

I am interested in low-dimensional topology and geometry from a computational point of view. In particular in my PhD, I am studying graphs embedded on surfaces and I am looking for proper ways to decompose them.
